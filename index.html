Most clients start seeing inquiries within 7–14 days of ads going live. Our target is 15+ qualified inquiries within your first 30 days.

Very minimal. We visit your clinic for one shoot day per month. You just need to be present (or a team member). We handle scripting, shooting, editing, and posting — everything.

Leads land directly in your WhatsApp Business inbox or Instagram DMs — in real time. You talk to the patient directly. No lead management software needed unless you want it.

No — the ad budget of ₹8,000–₹12,000/month is separate and goes directly to Meta (Facebook/Instagram). Our fee covers management, strategy, content, and execution. This way you have full transparency on where every rupee goes.
